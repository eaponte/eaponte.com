About

I have been involved with technology, software, and hardware most of my life. I started my career in IT as a computer technician, having built and repaired hundreds of computers over many years.

I then migrated into technical support, working in a variety of industries including systems and networking sales, computer and electronics manufacturing, and healthcare. Working in healthcare IT, I worked in hospitals, cancer centers, and home healthcare industries. Working in these industries allowed me to move into software support and QA.

After years of working in software support and QA, I became self-taught in HTML and CSS, and later taking front-end development coursework at the New York Code and Design Academy in the financial district of New York City.

I now actively work in web development, building sites for a variety of clients using HTML, CSS, Sass, Javascript, jQuery, and React JS, with a little .NET Core and ASP.NET thrown in.
